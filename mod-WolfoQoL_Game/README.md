Changes are Server-Side and compatible with people who dont have the mod.

## Features
Items that fall of the map will teleport back up.

Drones that die over pits or clipped through the ground will teleport back up.

Radar Scanner Interactable is more common.

Primoridal Teleporter can be interacted with after completion.

Lava Damage is now the same for enemies and player allies. 

Destination Portal on Prime Meridian wont steal your money, so that you can repair your drones more freely.

More entirely non-damaging moves dont put you into combat.

## Gamemodes

Prismatic Trial available with random seeds.

Eclipse Runs can now use Artifact of Dissonance, Prestige, and some other challenge artifacts.
